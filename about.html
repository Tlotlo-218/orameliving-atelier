Our Essence of Calm

Oramè Living began as a quiet collaboration between two friends who shared a vision: to create home fragrances that celebrate intentional living. In 2024, what started as thoughtful experiments in a small kitchen—blending natural oils, testing scent combinations, hand-pouring candles—evolved into a full-fledged brand driven by the belief that scent has the profound power to shape mood, memory, and the atmosphere of our most intimate spaces.

Our founders discovered that the fragrance industry often overlooks what matters most: connection to place, respect for natural ingredients, and the quiet art of creating a home that feels truly personal. They believed there was a missing gap in the market for fragrances that didn't just smell beautiful, but that told a story—one rooted in slow living, mindfulness, and the beauty of everyday rituals.

Every Oramè Living collection is born from this philosophy. We source the finest botanical extracts, essential oils, and sustainable materials, working with suppliers who share our commitment to quality and ethical practice. From hand-poured soy candles to ceramic diffusers and bespoke aroma mists, each product is crafted with meticulous attention to detail. We believe that luxury is not about excess—it's about intentionality, authenticity, and creating moments that matter.

Our story is deeply rooted in the rhythms of slow living and the magic of Botswana's quiet spaces. We draw inspiration from soft evening light filtering through windows, the patter of rain on a calm evening, and the universal desire to create sanctuaries where we can truly rest and be ourselves. Every scent is designed to evoke emotion, anchor memory, and transform a room into a space that feels thoughtfully curated.

We are committed to sustainability and responsible sourcing. Our packaging is minimalist and recyclable, our production processes honor both people and planet, and our community partnerships reflect our belief that business should uplift. We support local artisans and ethical suppliers, ensuring that every ingredient and component in our products comes from a place of integrity.

Today, Oramè Living stands as a testament to the power of intention. We create fragrances for people who understand that home is more than a place—it's a feeling. Whether you are welcoming loved ones, creating a sanctuary for yourself, or seeking a meaningful gift, our products are designed to make ordinary moments feel warmer, gentler, and richer with sensory beauty. We invite you to join us in the art of living beautifully and thoughtfully.
